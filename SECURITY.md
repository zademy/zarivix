## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our software very seriously. If you find a security vulnerability, please do NOT open a public issue.

1.  **Do not** open a GitHub issue.
2.  Send an email to **security@zarivix.com** (or the maintainer's email).
3.  Include as much detail as possible: steps to reproduce, affected versions, and potential impact.

We will try to acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide a timeline for the fix.

### Policy on Disclosure

- You give us reasonable time to investigate and mitigate the issue before making it public.
- We will prefer to coordinate the public announcement with you.
